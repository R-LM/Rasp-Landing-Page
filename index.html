The New, Innovative and Delicious Raspberry and Coconut milk soda

What is Rasp?

The Ingredients

Rasp is a soda made of 2 main ingridients: Raspberry juice and Coconut milk. Combining the right amount of this 2 natural and fresh ingridients with carbonated water creates a remarkable taste.


The Taste

The taste can be described as extremely refreshing, and with a unique and distinctive creamy flavor that, instead of saturating the drink, complements the freshness.

How was it made?

It started as an experiment

Raspberry and coconut milk smoothies are very delicious, but the team was trying to create something unique. They were trying to make a raspberry and coconut milk drink that was delicious but also fresh and thirst quenching.


It was a challenge

The main difficulty was balancing the richness of the coconut milk with the acidic flavor of the Raspberry and try to make it refreshing at the same time.

This combination of flavor works incredibly in smoothies, but the team was aiming for a refreshing drink. The ingredients have to be mixed in the right amount, at the right time and in the right way.


The team succeeded

The team was able to produce the result they wanted. The acidic flavor, the creaminess, that thirst quenching freshness, it was all there and it was delicious.

It was originally going to be sold as a non-carbonated soft drink, but, someone had the crazy idea to add fizziness to it.

The result was unbelievable.
